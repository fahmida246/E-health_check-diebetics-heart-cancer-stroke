myonil Myonil 50 is a muscle relaxer. It provides relief from the discomfort associated with acute, painful musculoskeletal conditions such as rigidity, tension, stiffness, and muscle spasms. Myonil 50 should be taken with food. This will prevent you from getting an upset stomach and reduce your chance of getting side effects.
